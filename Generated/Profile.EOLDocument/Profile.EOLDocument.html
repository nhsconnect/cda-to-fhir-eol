Version 1.0 : DRAFT C

    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    COFE-Document-Bundle-1BundleAn NHS Digital Bundle Profile to represent a container to collect a combination of the COFE Document resources.
    COFE-Composition-End-Of-Life-1CompositionEnd of Life Care Document Composition
    CareConnect-Encounter-1EncounterThe encounter resource represents an encounter between a care professional and the patient (or patient's record).
    CareConnect-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource represents the patient involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    CareConnect-Practitioner-1PractitionerThe Practitioner resource represents the healthcare professional directly or indirectly involved in the provision of healthcare related services.
    COFE-Related-Person-1RelatedPersonAn NHS Digital profile which carries information for a person with a relationship with the patient.
    CareConnect-Organization-1OrganizationThe Organization resource represents the organisation that employs the healthcare professional.
    COFE-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
    CareConnect-Condition-1ConditionThis Condition resource records detailed information about conditions or diagnoses recognised by a clinician.
    COFE-Medication-List-1ListAn NHS Digital profile for recording a snapshot of the list of Medications for the patient.
    CareConnect-MedicationStatement-1MedicationStatementThis MedicationStatement Resource is a record of a medication that is being consumed by a patient.
    CareConnect-Medication-1MedicationThis Medication Resource is primarily used for the identification and definition of a medication.
    CareConnect-Medication-Flag-1FlagThis Flag Resource carries prospective warnings of potential issues related to the patient's medications.
    COFE-Allergies-List-1ListAn NHS Digital profile for recording a snapshot of the list of Allergies for the patient.
    CareConnect-AllergyIntolerance-1AllergyIntoleranceThis AllergyIntolerance Resource records Risk of harmful or undesirable, physiological response which is unique to an individual and associated with exposure to a substance.
    COFE-Allergy-Flag-1FlagAn NHS Digital profile used to record prospective warnings of potential issues related to the patient's allergies.
    CareConnect-Observation-1ObservationThe Observation resource is used for tracking the current and historical observations that have been made for a patient.
    CareConnect-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
    CareConnect-Procedure-1ProcedureAn action that is or was performed on a patient.
    COFE-Device-1DeviceAn NHS Digital profile which identifies an instance of a manufactured item that is used in the provision of healthcare without being substantially changed through that activity. The device may be a medical or non-medical device.
    COFE-DNACPRDecision-QuestionnaireResponse-1QuestionnaireResponseThis QuestionnaireResponse Resource carries answers to questions regarding a patients decision on cardio-pulmonary resuscitation.
    COFE-EndOfLifeCarePlan-CarePlan-1CarePlanThis Care Plan resource represents a patients End of Life Care Plan.
    COFE-LastingPowerofAttorney-Observation-1ObservationLasting power of attorney indicator.
    COFE-LegalStatus-Observation-1ObservationFinding of the legal status of the patient.
    COFE-Prognosis-Observation-1ObservationThis Observation Resource represents a patients prognosis that is part of their crisis care plan.
    COFE-Risk-Flag-1FlagThis Flag Resource represents risks. Risks include to self, to others, from others and clinical risks.
    COFE-Services-CarePlan-1CarePlanAny care provision or services that are currently being provided to the patient.
    CareConnect-MedicationOrder-1MedicationOrderThis MedicationOrder Resource represents an order for both supply of the medication and the instructions for administration of the medication to a patient.
